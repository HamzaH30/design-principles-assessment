    About Mughals & Co. Restaurant

    Inspired by the Mughlai cuisine found in the vast lands of the Mughal Empire, Mughals & Co. has been serving authentic Mughlai dishes to the public for over 50 years!

    The Mughal Empire was an early modern Islamic empire situated in South Asia. They ruled the Indian subcontinent for over 2 centuries. Arts, trades, literature, and especially architecture, flourished within the empire. Many sites built in the Mughal Empire are now UNESCO World Heritage Sites, the most popular being the Taj Mahal, described as the “jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage.”

    To read more about the Mughal Empire, click this link.

    Along with Mughal rule, came a cuisine rich in colour, spices, aroma, and taste. Recipes that were perfected over time by skilled chefs and prepared only for the Emperors and Royalty have now become the icon of South Asian cuisine. Many common Indian foods such as biryani, kebabs, kofte, and pilau all originate from Mughlai cuisine.

    Mughlai cooking was a unique style of cooking that was unbeknownst to the world at the time. It included spices and herbs found only in the corners of the empire and used a variety of cooking techniques from all over the empire. The curries and stews are mixed with the purest butter, milk, and cream. The traditional Mughlai desserts are elegantly spiced with saffron, cardamom, and cinnamon.
